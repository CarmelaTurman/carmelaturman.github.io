Round 41 – Purple Petals

Join yarn with a standing single crochet in the fourth sc made into the first ch-5 space of any short side. Sc in the next 3 stitches. Fpdc around the fpsc from Round 39. Sc in the next stitch of Round 40 directly behind the fpdc. Sc in the next 3 stitches.

Short Side: 4 sc, fpdc around the fpsc from Round 39, sc in next stitch of Round 40, sc in next 3 stitches.

* Long Side: Sc in next stitch and mark this stitch. (Skip 3 stitches, make 4 tr in next stitch, ch 3, sl st into third ch from hook to form a picot, make 4 tr in same stitch, skip 3 stitches, sc in next stitch) 10 times. Mark the last sc of this long side.

You should now have 10 petals separated by 1 single crochet each.

Repeat from * to * 3 more times, omitting the last short side on the final repeat. Join to the first sc with a sl st. Fasten off.

Stitch Count

Total: 400 stitches and 40 picots

Per Short Side: 9 stitches

Per Long Side: 10 petals and 11 single crochets

Round 42

Join with a standing single crochet in the seventh treble of the last petal on any long side. (Ch 1, sc) in same stitch. Sc in last tr of petal and hdc in next stitch. Sc in next 9 stitches. The fifth sc falls in the top of the fpdc from the previous round. Hdc in next stitch. Sc in first tr of next petal. (Sc, ch 1, sc) in next tr.

* Long Side (working in blo): (Ch 3, skip next 4 tr and picot, sc in blo of seventh tr, hdc in blo of last tr, fpdc around the sc between petals, hdc in blo of first tr of next petal, sc in blo of second tr) 9 times. Ch 3, skip next 4 tr and picot.

Short Side: (Sc, ch 1, sc) in seventh tr of last petal. Sc in last tr and hdc in next stitch. Sc in next 9 stitches. Hdc in next stitch. Sc in first tr of next petal. (Sc, ch 1, sc) in next tr.

Repeat from * to * 3 more times, omitting last short side on final repeat. Join with a sl st.

Stitch Count

Total: 248 stitches, 40 ch-3 spaces, 8 ch-1 corners

Per Short Side: 15 stitches

Per Long Side: 47 stitches and 10 ch-3 spaces

Round 43

If using same colour: (ch 1, sc, ch 1, sc) in same stitch as join. If using new colour, join with a standing single crochet in the last sc of any long side, (ch 1, sc) in same stitch.

Sc in the next ch-1 space. Skip the next stitch and sc in the next 7 stitches. The last sc falls in the sc made into the fpdc from Round 41. Fpdc around that fpdc. Sc in the next 7 stitches. Sc in the next ch-1 space. (Sc, ch 1, sc) in next stitch.

* Long Side: 2 sc in next ch-3 space. Sc in next 5 stitches. (3 sc in next ch-3 space, sc in next 5 stitches) 8 times. 2 sc in last ch-3 space.

Short Side: (Sc, ch 1, sc) in next stitch. Sc in next ch-1 space. Skip next stitch, sc in next 7 stitches. Fpdc around fpdc from Round 41. Sc in next 7 stitches. Sc in ch-1 space. (Sc, ch 1, sc) in next stitch.

Repeat from * to * 3 more times, omitting last short side. Join with sl st.

Stitch Count

Total: 376 stitches and 8 ch-1 corners

Per Short Side: 19 stitches

Per Long Side: 75 sc

Round 44

If using same colour: (ch 1, sc, ch 1, sc) in same stitch as join. If using new colour, join with a standing sc in same stitch, (ch 1, sc) in same stitch.

Sc in next ch-1 space. Sc in next 19 stitches. Sc in next ch-1 space. (Sc, ch 1, sc) in next stitch.

* Long Side: Skip next sc. Sc in next 4 stitches. (Fpdc around fpdc from previous round, skip next stitch, sc in next 7 stitches) 8 times. Fpdc, skip next stitch, sc in next 3 stitches.

Short Side: (Sc, ch 1, sc) in next stitch. Sc in next ch-1 space. Sc in next 19 stitches. Sc in next ch-1 space. (Sc, ch 1, sc) in next stitch.

Repeat from * to * 3 more times, omitting last short side. Join.

Stitch Count

Total: 380 stitches and 8 ch-1 corners

Per Short Side: 23 sc

Per Long Side: 74 stitches

Round 45

If using same colour, sl st into ch-1 space, ch 1 and make 2 sc in same space. If changing colour, join with a standing sc in same space and sc again.

Skip first stitch. Sc in next 11 stitches. Fpdc around fpdc from Round 43. Sc in next 11 stitches. Make 2 sc in next ch-1 space.

* Long Side: Skip next stitch. Catch the picot from Round 41 with the next hdc (working through picot and next stitch). (Hdc in next 7 stitches, the 4th hdc in fpdc, catch next picot with hdc) 9 times.

Short Side: 2 sc in next ch-1 space, skip next stitch, sc in next 11 stitches, fpdc around fpdc, sc in next 11 stitches, 2 sc in next ch-1 space.

Repeat from * to * 3 more times, omitting last short side. Join.

Stitch Count

Total: 400 stitches

Per Short Side: 27 stitches

Per Long Side: 73 hdc

Special Stitches

Popcorn Stitch

Make 5 dc in the same stitch. Remove your hook from the last stitch and insert your hook into the first dc of the group. Grab the last stitch with your hook and pull it through the first stitch, then ch 1.

Standing Stitches

Standing sc, hdc, and dc are stitches used to start a round without a beginning chain.

Beginning Treble Bobble

Harmony-Mandala-Blanket/images/trebblebobble.jpg

Ch 4. Yarn over twice and insert hook into the stitch at the base of the ch-4. Yarn over and pull up a loop. (Yarn over and pull through 2 loops) twice, leaving 2 loops on the hook. Yarn over twice again and insert hook into the same stitch. Yarn over and pull up a loop, then (yarn over and pull through 2 loops) twice, leaving 3 loops on the hook. Yarn over and pull through all loops.

Round 46

If using the same colour, (ch 1, sc, ch 1, sc) in the same stitch.

If using a new colour, join with a standing sc in the same stitch as the slip stitch join. (Ch 1, sc) in the same stitch. This is the first corner.

Sc in the next 26 stitches. (Sc, ch 1, sc) in the next stitch.

Long Side:

Skip the next hdc. Hdc in the next 3 stitches. (Fptr around the fpdc from Round 44, skip the next stitch of Round 45, hdc in the next 7 stitches) 8 times. Fptr around the fpdc from Round 44. Skip the next stitch of Round 45 and hdc in the next 3 stitches.

Short Side:

(Sc, ch 1, sc) in the next stitch. Sc in the next 26 stitches. (Sc, ch 1, sc) in the next stitch.

Repeat from * to * three more times, omitting the last short side on the final repeat. Join with a slip stitch.

Stitch Count

Total: 404 stitches and 8 ch-1 corners

Per Short Side: 28 sc

Per Long Side: 73 stitches

Round 47

If using the same colour, slip stitch into the ch-1 space and make (ch 1, sc). If changing colour, join with a standing sc in the same space.

Sc in the next 14 stitches. Fpdc around the fpdc from Round 45. Sc in the next 14 stitches. Sc in the next ch-1 space.

Long Side: Ch 1. (Fptr around the picot from Round 41, skip next stitch of Round 46, hdc in the next 7 stitches) 9 times. Fptr around the picot, ch 1, skip next stitch.

Short Side: Sc in the next ch-1 space. Sc in the next 14 stitches. Fpdc around the fpdc from Round 45. Sc in the next 14 stitches. Sc in the next ch-1 space.

Repeat three more times, omitting last short side. Join with a slip stitch. Fasten off.

Stitch Count

Total: 416 stitches and 8 ch-1 spaces

Per Short Side: 31 stitches

Per Long Side: 73 stitches

Round 48 – Leaves

Join yarn with a standing sc in any ch-1 corner space. (Ch 1, sc) in the same space.

Short Side: Skip first stitch and sc in blo of next stitch. (Ch 4, skip 4 stitches, sc in blo) twice. Slip stitch in next stitch.

Make a Beginning Treble Bobble in the same stitch as the slip stitch. Sc around the fpdc from Round 45. Make another Beginning Treble Bobble in that sc. Skip the next 6 stitches and slip stitch in next. (Sc in blo, ch 4, skip next 4 stitches) twice. Sc in blo of next.

Long Side:

(Sc, ch 1, sc) in next corner space. Skip fptr and sc in next 4 stitches. (Fptr, skip next stitch, sc in next 7 stitches) 8 times. Fptr, skip next stitch, sc in last 3 stitches. (Sc, ch 1, sc) in next ch-1 space.

Repeat three more times, omitting last corner. Join with slip stitch.

Stitch Count

Total: 360 stitches, 8 leaves

Round 49

If using same colour, slip stitch into the ch-1 space and make (ch 1, sc, ch 1, sc). If changing colour, join with a standing sc.

Short Side: Skip first stitch. Sc in flo of next stitch. (Working in front, sc in the 4 skipped stitches from Round 47, sc in flo of next) twice. Skip next stitch. Working behind the first leaf, sc in next 3 stitches. Hdc between leaves. Sc in next 3. Skip next stitch. Repeat same sequence once more.

Long Side: (Sc, ch 1, sc) in corner. Fptr around the fpdc of Round 47. Skip 3 stitches, dc in next 2. (Ch 1, skip 1, dc in next 2, fptr around next fptr, dc in next, fptr around same, dc in next 2) 8 times. Ch 1, skip 1, dc in next 2, fptr, skip next 2, (sc, ch 1, sc).

Repeat 3 more times, omitting final corner. Join with slip stitch.

Stitch Count

Total: 380 stitches and 44 ch-1 spaces

Round 50 – Tulips

If using same colour, slip stitch into ch-1 space and make (ch 1, sc, ch 1, sc). If changing colour, join with a standing sc.

Skip first stitch, sc in next 30 stitches. (Sc, ch 1, sc) in corner.

Long Side: Skip first stitch, sc in next 3. (Popcorn stitch in skipped fptr of Round 48, sc in next 7 stitches) 8 times. Popcorn stitch, sc in next 3, skip next stitch.

Repeat around, skipping final short side. Join with slip stitch.

Stitch Count

Total: 420 stitches

Per Short Side: 32 sc

Per Long Side: 73 stitches
