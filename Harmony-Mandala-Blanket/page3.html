Round 1
Into a magic ring: ch 4 (this counts as your first dc and ch 1) and then make a dc
(Ch 2, dc, ch 1, dc) five times. Ch 2 and join to the third st of the beginning ch-4 with a sl st.

Stitch Count: 12 dc’s, 6 ch-2 spaces and 6 ch-1 spaces
Stitch Count: 12 dc, 6 ch-2 spaces, 6 ch-1 spaces
Round 2
If using the same colour for Round 2:
  - Sl st into the next ch-1 space, ch 1 and sc in the same space.

If using a new colour for Round 2:
  - Join your new yarn by making a standing single crochet in any ch-1 space around.

Repeat:
  (Make 5 dc’s in the next ch-2 space {Photo 2}, sc in the next ch-1 space) five times.
  Make 5 dc’s in the last ch-2 space {Photo 4}.
  Join to the first sc with a sl st.

Stitch Count: 30 dc’s and 6 sc’s {6 Petals}
Stitch Count: 30 dc, 6 sc (6 petals)
Round 3
If using the same colour:
  - Ch 1 and make a fpsc around the post of the same sc.

If using a new colour:
  - Join with a standing sc in any sc (around).

Pattern:
  *(Dc, ch 1) in the next 4 dc’s. Dc in the last dc {Photo 3}. Fpsc in the next sc {Photo 4}.*
  Repeat from * to * 5 more times, omitting the last fpsc on the last repeat.
  Join to the top of the first sc with a sl st.

Stitch Count: 30 dc’s, 6 fpsc’s, and 24 ch-1 spaces {6 Petals}
Stitch Count: 30 dc, 6 fpsc, 24 ch-1 spaces
Round 4
Note: round 4 requires concentration — it's easy to miss ch-1's or place stitches in the wrong spot. Your flower may buckle; that's normal.
        
If using the same colour:
  - Ch 1 and fpsc around the post of the same fpsc as the sl st join.

If using a new colour:
  - Start with a fpsc around the post of any fpsc {Photo 1}.

Repeat:
  *Hdc in the first dc. (This first dc is right next to the fpsc.)
   (Ch 1, dc in the next dc, ch 1, dc in the next ch-1 space) twice.
   Ch 1 and dc in the next dc {Photo 2}.
   Ch 1 and hdc in the last dc.
   Fpsc in the next fpsc {Photo 3}.*
  Repeat from * to * 5 more times, omitting the last fpsc on the last repeat.
  Join to the first fpsc with a sl st. Fasten off and change colours.

Stitch Counts (Total):
  48 st’s and 36 ch-1 spaces {6 fpsc’s, 12 hdc’s, 30 dc’s, 36 ch-1 spaces}

Per Petal:
  2 hdc’s, 5 dc’s, and 6 ch-1 spaces per petal {1 fpsc between petals}
Total: 48 sts, 36 ch-1 spaces (per petal: 2 hdc, 5 dc, 6 ch-1)
Round 5
*Ch 2 and fpdc around the post of the fpsc between the petals.
 (Ch 2, bpsc around the post of the next dc) 5 times.*
Repeat from * to * 5 more times. Omit the last bpsc of the last repeat.
Join to the first bpsc with a sl st.

Stitch Counts:
  Total: 30 bpsc’s, 6 fpdc’s, and 36 ch-2 spaces
  Per Petal: 5 bpsc’s and 6 ch-2 spaces per petal, and 1 fpdc between petals
Total: 30 bpsc, 6 fpdc, 36 ch-2 spaces
Round 6
Start:
  - Standing double crochet in the next ch-2 space.
  - Ch 2 and dc in the next ch-2 space. (These 2 dc’s should fall on either side of the fpdc from previous round.)

Repeat pattern:
  *Ch 2 and hdc in the next ch-2 space.
   (Ch 2, sc in the next ch-2 space) twice.
   Ch 2, hdc in the next ch-2 space.
   (Ch 2, dc in the next ch-2 space) twice {Photo 2}.*

Repeat from * to * 4 more times.

Finish:
  Ch 2 and hdc in the next ch-2 space.
  (Ch 2, sc in the next ch-2 space) twice.
  Ch 2 and hdc in the next ch-2 space.
  Ch 2 and join to the third ch of the beginning ch-5 (or the standing double crochet) with a sl st.

Note:
  The dc’s fall on either side of the fpdc’s from the previous round.

Stitch Count: 36 st’s and 36 ch-2 spaces {12 sc’s, 12 hdc’s, 12 dc’s}
Stitch Count: 36 sts; 36 ch-2 spaces (12 sc, 12 hdc, 12 dc)
Round 7
If using the same colour:
  - Sl st into the next ch-2 space. Ch 1 and make 2 sc’s in the same ch-2 space.

If using a different colour:
  - Start with a standing sc in the next ch-2 space and then make another sc in the same ch-2 space.

Pattern:
  Make 2 sc’s in the next ch-2 space.
  Make 3 sc’s in the next ch-2 space.
  *Make 2 sc’s in each of the next 2 ch-2 spaces, make 3 sc’s in the next ch-2 space.*
  Repeat from * to * 10 more times.
  Join to the BLO of the first sc with a sl st.

Stitch Count: 84 sc’s — count your stitches carefully!
Stitch Count: 84 sc (BLO join)
Round 8
Working in the back loops only (BLO) for this round.

If using the same colour:
  - Ch 2 (this counts as your first hdc).

If using a different colour:
  - Start with a standing hdc in the BLO of the same st as the sl st join.

Pattern:
  Hdc in the BLO of the next 5 st’s and make 2 hdc’s in the BLO of the next st {Photo 2}.
  *Hdc in the BLO of the next 6 st’s, make 2 hdc’s in the BLO of the next st.* 
  Repeat from * to * 10 more times.
  Join to the top of the beginning ch-2 (or the standing hdc) with a sl st.

Stitch Count: 96 hdc’s (worked into the BLO)
Stitch Count: 96 hdc (BLO)
Special Stitches & Notes
Picot:
  - Ch 3, sl st in the third chain from the hook.

Standing Stitches:
  - Standing sc, hdc, dc: used to start a round without beginning chains.

V-stitch:
  - (Dc, ch 2, dc) in the same stitch.

Invisible Join:
  - If preferred over sl st joins, see a photo tutorial (link placeholder).
  - WARNING: If using the invisible join, be sure you understand which stitch to join to and which stitch to start your next round in, otherwise the mandala will be uneven.

Round 9
If using the same colour:
  - ch 5 (counts as the first dc and ch-2) and then dc in the same st. This is your first V-stitch made. Ch 1.

If using a different colour:
  - Start with a standing double crochet in the same stitch as the sl st join, then ch 2 and dc in the same st. This is your first V-stitch made. Ch 1.

Pattern:
  *Skip 3 st’s, make a V-stitch in the next st, ch 1.*
  Repeat from * to * 22 more times.
  Join to the third ch of the beginning ch-5 (or top of the standing dc) with a sl st.

Stitch Count: 24 V-stitches and 24 ch-1 spaces between V-stitches 
  {48 dc’s, 24 ch-1 spaces, and 24 ch-2 spaces}
Stitch Count: 24 V-stitches; 48 dc; 24 ch-1; 24 ch-2
Round 10
Note: Only work into the ch-2 space (between the legs of each V-stitch). Skip all the ch-1 spaces between V-stitches.

If using the same colour:
  - Sl st in the next ch-2 space, ch 1 and sc in the same space.

If using a different colour:
  - Start with a standing single crochet in the next ch-2 space.

Pattern:
  *Make 4 tr’s into the next ch-2 space. Ch 3 and sl st in the third ch from the hook (creates a picot at the top of the 4th tr).
   Make 4 more tr’s into the same ch-2 space. Sc in the next ch-2 space.*
  Repeat from * to * 11 more times.
  Omit the last sc on the last repeat.
  Join to the first sc with a sl st. Fasten off and change colours.

Hint: If you have more than 12 petals at the end of this round, you haven't skipped the ch-1 spaces between V-stitches.

Stitch Counts:
  Total: 12 petals and 12 sc’s {96 tr’s and 12 sc’s}
  Per Petal: 8 tr’s, 1 picot, 1 sc between petals
Total: 12 petals; 96 tr; 12 sc (per petal: 8 tr + 1 picot)
