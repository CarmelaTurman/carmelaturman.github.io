Rounds 1–10 | Harmony Mandala Blanket

Follow these steps to create the first ten rounds of your vibrant Harmony Mandala Blanket. Each round introduces textures, petals, and color changes to make your blanket a cozy, meditative masterpiece!

Round 1
Into a magic ring: ch 4 (counts as first dc + ch 1), then make a dc
(Ch 2, dc, ch 1, dc) five times. Ch 2 and join to third st of beginning ch-4 with sl st.

Stitch Count: 12 dc’s, 6 ch-2 spaces, 6 ch-1 spaces
12 dc, 6 ch-2 spaces, 6 ch-1 spaces
Round 2
Same colour:
  - Sl st into next ch-1 space, ch 1, sc in same space.

New colour:
  - Join new yarn with standing sc in any ch-1 space.

Repeat:
  (5 dc in next ch-2 space, sc in next ch-1 space) five times.
  5 dc in last ch-2 space.
  Join with sl st.

Stitch Count: 30 dc, 6 sc (6 petals)
30 dc, 6 sc
Round 3
Same colour:
  - Ch 1, fpsc around post of same sc.

New colour:
  - Join with standing sc in any sc.

Pattern:
  *(Dc, ch 1) in next 4 dc, Dc in last dc, fpsc in next sc.*
  Repeat 5 more times, omit last fpsc.
  Join to top of first sc.

Stitch Count: 30 dc, 6 fpsc, 24 ch-1 spaces (6 petals)
30 dc, 6 fpsc, 24 ch-1
Round 4
Round 4 may buckle; this is normal.
Same colour:
  - Ch 1, fpsc around post of same fpsc as sl st join.

New colour:
  - Start with fpsc around post of any fpsc.

Repeat:
  *Hdc in first dc.
   (Ch 1, dc in next dc, ch 1, dc in next ch-1) twice.
   Ch 1, dc in next dc.
   Ch 1, hdc in last dc.
   Fpsc in next fpsc.*
  Repeat 5 more times, omit last fpsc.
  Join to first fpsc. Fasten off/change colour.

Stitch Counts: 48 sts, 36 ch-1 spaces (per petal: 2 hdc, 5 dc, 6 ch-1)
48 sts, 36 ch-1
Round 5
Ch 2, fpdc around post of fpsc between petals.
(Ch 2, bpsc around post of next dc) 5 times.
Repeat 5 more times, omit last bpsc.
Join to first bpsc.

Total: 30 bpsc, 6 fpdc, 36 ch-2 spaces
Per petal: 5 bpsc, 6 ch-2, 1 fpdc between petals
30 bpsc, 6 fpdc, 36 ch-2
Round 6
Start:
  - Standing dc in next ch-2 space.
  - Ch 2, dc in next ch-2 space (on either side of previous fpdc).

Repeat pattern:
  *Ch 2, hdc in next ch-2 space.
   (Ch 2, sc in next ch-2) twice.
   Ch 2, hdc in next ch-2.
   (Ch 2, dc in next ch-2) twice.*
Repeat 4 more times.

Finish:
  Ch 2, hdc in next ch-2.
  (Ch 2, sc in next ch-2) twice.
  Ch 2, hdc in next ch-2.
  Ch 2, join with sl st.

Stitch Count: 36 sts, 36 ch-2 (12 sc, 12 hdc, 12 dc)
36 sts, 36 ch-2
Round 7
Same colour:
  - Sl st into next ch-2 space. Ch 1, 2 sc in same space.

New colour:
  - Standing sc in next ch-2 space, then 1 more sc in same space.

Pattern:
  2 sc in next ch-2 space.
  3 sc in next ch-2 space.
  *2 sc in each of next 2 ch-2 spaces, 3 sc in next ch-2 space.*
Repeat 10 more times. Join to BLO of first sc.

Stitch Count: 84 sc
84 sc
Round 8
Working in back loops only (BLO).

Same colour:
  - Ch 2 (counts as first hdc).

New colour:
  - Standing hdc in BLO of same st.

Pattern:
  Hdc in BLO of next 5 sts, 2 hdc in BLO of next st.
  *Hdc in BLO of next 6 sts, 2 hdc in next st.* Repeat 10 more times.
  Join to top of beginning ch-2 or standing hdc.

Stitch Count: 96 hdc (BLO)
96 hdc
Round 9
Same colour:
  - Ch 5 (first dc + ch-2), dc in same st. First V-stitch. Ch 1.

New colour:
  - Standing dc in same st, ch 2, dc in same st.

Pattern:
  *Skip 3 sts, V-stitch in next st, ch 1.* Repeat 22 more times.
  Join to top of beginning ch-5 or standing dc.

Stitch Count: 24 V-stitches, 48 dc, 24 ch-1, 24 ch-2
24 V-st, 48 dc, 24 ch-1, 24 ch-2
Round 10
Only work into ch-2 spaces.

Same colour:
  - Sl st into next ch-2 space, ch 1, sc in same space.

New colour:
  - Standing sc in next ch-2 space.

Pattern:
  *4 tr in next ch-2, ch 3, sl st in third ch (picot), 4 tr in same ch-2, sc in next ch-2.*
Repeat 11 more times. Omit last sc. Join with sl st. Fasten off/change colour.

Stitch Counts:
  12 petals, 96 tr, 12 sc
Per Petal: 8 tr, 1 picot, 1 sc between petals
12 petals; 96 tr; 12 sc
