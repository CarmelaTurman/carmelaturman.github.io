Color Planning & Layout

Proper color planning is key to a beautiful Harmony Circle Mandala Blanket. Use this guide to choose colors for each section and ensure smooth transitions across your mandala.

Color Key by Round Sections

Rounds 1–5: Cream
Rounds 6–10: Gold
Rounds 11–15: Coral
Rounds 16–20: Aqua
Rounds 21–25: Lavender
Rounds 26–30: Mint
Rounds 31–36: Rose
Rounds 37–42: Teal
Rounds 43–45: Plum
Rounds 46–50: Soft Rose
Rounds 51–55: White
Rounds 56–60: Cream (final trim)

Tips for Planning Your Mandala

  • Lay out yarn in order before starting to visualize the gradient and flow.
  • Consider using a **color palette board** or drawing a circle diagram to plan color placement.
  • Use contrasting colors for textured rounds to make patterns stand out.
  • Keep the darkest or boldest colors for outer rounds to frame the mandala.
  • Optional: Take photos as you progress to check color balance and harmony.

Additional Tips

  • For beginners, keep colors simple and stick to the planned palette.
  • Advanced makers can experiment with variegated yarn or subtle gradients for extra depth.
  • Always have a small amount of extra yarn in each color for corrections or final adjustments.
