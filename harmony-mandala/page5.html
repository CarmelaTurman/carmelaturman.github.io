Rounds 11–15: Expanding Petals

Rounds 11–15: Coral & Rose

Ch 3, 2 dc in same st, skip 2, 3 dc in next; rep around (~16 petals)

Popcorn stitch in each petal tip; join (~16 popcorns)

Ch 4, tr in next st; rep around (~48 tr)

Ch 3, 5 dc in next st, skip 2; rep around (~16 shells)

Sc in each st around; join (~80 sc)
