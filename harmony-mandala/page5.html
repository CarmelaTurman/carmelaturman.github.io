Rounds 11–15: Coral Section

Rounds 11–15: Coral

Join Coral in any ch-3 sp. Ch 3, 2 dc in same sp. 3 dc in next ch-3 sp; rep around. Join. (108 dc)

Ch 3, 2 dc in next st, ch 1, skip 1, dc in next 2 sts; rep around. Join. (~120 sts)

Ch 1, sc in same st, ch 5, skip 2 sts, sc in next st; rep around. Join. (~40 ch-5 sps)

Sl st into first ch-5 sp, ch 3, 4 dc in same sp (5 dc total = shell). Sc in next ch-5 sp, 5 dc in next sp; rep around. Join. (20 shells)

Ch 1, sc between shells, ch 3; rep around. Join. (20 sc, 20 ch-3 sps)
