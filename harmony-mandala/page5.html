Round 21

If using the same colour: ch 3 (counts as first dc). Dc in the next st.

If using a different colour: make a standing double crochet in the same st as the sl st join. Dc in the next st.

Repeat:
*Make 2 dc in the next st. Dc in the next 3 st’s (last dc should fall in the dc after the fpdtr from previous round). (Ch 2 and make a cluster) three times. Ch 2 and dc in the next 3 st’s.* Repeat from * to * 11 more times, omitting the last 2 dc’s on the final repeat. Join to the top of the beginning ch-3 (or standing dc) with a sl st.

Stitch Count: 132 st’s and 48 ch-2 spaces (96 dc’s and 36 clusters)

Round 22

This round forms the points of the petals. Curling is normal and will correct in the next round.

If using the same colour: ch 1, sc in the same st, sc in the next 2 st’s.
If using a different colour: make a standing sc in the same st as the sl st join, sc in the next 2 st’s.

Repeat:
*Fpdtr around the fpdtr from Round 20 to the left of the popcorn (right if left-handed). Fpdtr around the fpdtr from Round 20 to the right of the popcorn (left if left-handed). Skip next st of Round 21. Sc in next 3 st’s. (Make 2 sc in next ch-2 space, sc in top of next cluster) three times. Make 2 sc in last ch-2 space. Sc in next 4 st’s.* Repeat 11 more times, omitting the last 3 sc’s on the final repeat. Join with a sl st.

Stitch Count: 240 st’s (216 sc’s and 24 fpdtr’s)

Round 23

Note: When working around the fpdtr’s from the previous round, make ONE fpdc around both posts together and skip the tops of those stitches.

If using the same colour: ch 3 (counts as dc), dc in next 2 st’s.
If using a different colour: standing dc in same st as sl st join, dc in next 2 st’s.

Repeat:
*Fpdc around both fpdtr posts from Round 22. Dc in next 18 sc’s (do not skip the first sc after the fpdc).* Repeat 11 more times, omitting the last 3 dc’s on the final repeat. Join with sl st.

Stitch Count: 228 st’s (216 dc’s and 12 fpdc’s)

Round 24

This round adds increases by working into the tops of fpdc’s and around their posts.

If using the same colour: ch 1, sc in same st, sc next st.
If using a different colour: standing sc in same st as sl st join, sc next st.

Repeat:
*Hdc in next 2 st’s (last hdc goes into the top of fpdc). Fpdc around the fpdc post. Hdc in next 2 st’s. Sc in next 15 st’s.* Repeat 11 more times, omitting the last 2 sc’s on the final repeat. Join with sl st and fasten off.

Stitch Count: 240 st’s (180 sc’s, 48 hdc’s, and 12 fpdc’s)

Round 25 (Optional)

This round is optional. The crab stitch tends to make a lot of people “crabby”, but I quite like making it. Don’t worry if your mandala starts wobbling slightly when you make the crab stitch round. Once you’ve blocked it, it will be right as rain.

Start in any stitch around with a standing single crochet (Photo 1). Make a crab stitch in each stitch around (Photos 2 and 3).

Special Stitches

Tr Cluster: Tr3tog worked around front posts (not stitch tops).
Standing Stitches: Standing sc, hdc, and dc to start rounds without chains.

Round 26

In this round, stitches are worked around back posts. Take care not to skip or double-wrap posts.

Join with a standing sc around back post of any fpdc from Round 24. Bpsc next 9 st’s.

Repeat:
*Bphdc in next 10 st’s (last into st before fpdc). Bpdc next 9 st’s. Make (3 bptr, ch 2, 3 bptr) in next st for corner. Bpdc next 9 st’s (last before fpdc). Bphdc next 10 st’s. Bpsc next 20 st’s.* Repeat 3 more times, omitting last 10 bpsc’s on final repeat. Join with sl st.

Stitch Count:
Total: 256 st’s + 4 ch-2 spaces
Per side: 64 st’s (20 bpsc, 20 bphdc, 18 bpdc, 6 bptr)

Round 27

If using same colour: ch 1, sc in same st, sc next 13 st’s.
If using new colour: standing sc in same st as sl st join, sc next 13 st’s.

Round 28

When you make the corners in this round, they will seem too tight for the round. Do not worry about it.

Join your yarn by making a standing single crochet in any ch-3 picot around (Photo 1).

Ch 4. Make a tr cluster, working around the front posts of the 3 bptr’s in Round 26 (Photos 3 and 4). Ch 2 (Photo 5). Skip the first 4 dc’s after the bptr’s. Hdc in the next 2 stitches (indicated with an arrow in Photo 6 and illustrated in Photo 7). Ch 2, skip the next 2 stitches and hdc in the next 2 stitches. (Ch 2, skip the next 2 stitches and sc in the next 2 stitches) 9 times. (Ch 2, skip the next 2 stitches and hdc in the next 2 stitches) twice. There should now be 4 dc’s left before the bptr’s from Round 26 (Photo 8). Ch 2. Make a tr cluster by working around the front posts of the 3 bptr’s in Round 26 (Photo 9). Ch 4. Sc in the picot (Photo 10).

Repeat this sequence 3 more times, omitting the last sc on the last repeat. Join to the top of the first sc with a slip stitch. Fasten off and work away your tails of yarn.

Once you have completed the round, push the (ch-4, sc, ch-4) parts in each corner to the back of your work, never to be seen or used again (Photos 11 and 12)!

Stitch Counts

Total: 116 stitches, 8 ch-4 spaces, and 56 ch-2 spaces (8 tr clusters, 32 hdc’s, and 76 sc’s – including corner sc’s)

Per Side: 29 stitches, 2 ch-4 spaces, and 14 ch-2 spaces (2 tr clusters, 8 hdc’s, and 19 sc’s – including corner sc)

Round 29

Most stitches are worked into Round 27, except for 16. When working into stitches under ch-2 spaces, work in front of the chains.

Join with a standing hdc in the second hdc from Round 28 on any side.

Repeat:
*Fptr in 2 st’s from Round 27 under next ch-2 space. (Ch 2, fpdc in 2 st’s from Round 27 under next ch-2 space) × 10. Ch 2, fptr in next 2 st’s. Hdc in next 2 hdc’s from Round 28. Ch 2, tr cluster in first 3 dc’s of half-circle. (Ch 4, tr cluster in next 3 dc’s) × 3. Ch 2, hdc in next 2 hdc’s from Round 28.* Repeat 3 more times, omitting last hdc on final repeat. Join with sl st.

Stitch Count:
Total: 128 st’s, 12 ch-4 spaces, 52 ch-2 spaces (16 tr clusters, 16 hdc, 16 fptr, 80 fpdc)
Per side: 32 st’s, 3 ch-4 spaces, 13 ch-2 spaces

Round 29

Except for 16 stitches, you will be working only into the stitches of Round 27 in this round. When working into the stitches from Round 27 below the ch-2 spaces, work in front of the ch-2 spaces, not over them!

You will notice that there is a fpdc in the instructions below that has been highlighted in the original pattern. This is because a lot of people have missed the fact that those stitches should be fpdc’s, not fptr’s. If you accidentally make them fptr’s your square will simply bulge slightly.

Join your yarn by making a standing half-double crochet in the second hdc from Round 28 on any side (Photo 1).

Fptr in the 2 stitches from Round 27 directly below the next ch-2 space (Photo 2). Remember to work in front of the ch-2 space, not over it (Photo 5). (Ch 2 and fpdc in the 2 stitches from Round 27 directly below the next ch-2 space (Photos 3 and 4)) 10 times. Ch 2 and fptr around the 2 stitches from Round 27 directly below the next ch-2 space. Hdc in the next 2 hdc’s from Round 28 (Photo 6).

You will now be working into the half-circle formed by the (6 dc, picot, 6 dc) from Round 27, and in front of the ch-4 spaces from Round 28. You will have to push the “leaves” out of the way to find the first and last stitches of the half-circle (Photo 7). Ch 2 and make a tr cluster in the first 3 dc’s of the half-circle (Photos 7 and 8). (Ch 4 and make a tr cluster in the next 3 dc’s of the half-circle) three times (Photos 9 and 10). Ch 2 and hdc in the next 2 hdc’s from Round 28 (Photo 11).

Repeat this sequence 3 more times, omitting the last hdc on the last repeat. Join to the top of the first hdc with a slip stitch.

Stitch Counts

Total: 128 stitches, 12 ch-4 spaces, and 52 ch-2 spaces (16 tr clusters, 16 hdc’s, 16 fptr’s, and 80 fpdc’s)

Per Side: 32 stitches, 3 ch-4 spaces, and 13 ch-2 spaces (4 tr clusters, 4 hdc’s, 4 fptr’s, and 20 fpdc’s)

Round 30

Join with a standing dc in the central ch-4 corner space. (2 dc, ch 2, 3 dc) in same space for first corner.

Repeat:
*5 dc in next ch-4 space. 5 dc in next ch-2 space. Skip next 2 hdc, sl st in next fptr. 8 dc in next ch-2 space. Skip next fpdc, sl st into next fpdc. Working into Round 28 behind Round 29: (Sc in next 2 sc, 2 sc in next ch-2 space) × 8. Sc in next 2 st’s. Sl st in next fpdc. Skip next fpdc, 8 dc in next ch-2 space. Skip next 2 fptr, sl st in next hdc. Skip next hdc, 5 dc in next ch-2. 5 dc in next ch-4 space. (3 dc, ch 2, 3 dc) in central corner ch-4 space.* Repeat 3 more times, omitting final corner on last repeat. Join with sl st.

Stitch Count:
Total: 304 st’s, 4 ch-2 spaces (168 dc and 136 sc)
Per side: 76 st’s (42 dc and 34 sc)
