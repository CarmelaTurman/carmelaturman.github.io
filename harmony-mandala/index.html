Harmony Mandala Journey

Welcome, brave crocheter, to the Harmony Mandala journey! Prepare for loops, twirls, and a meditative tangle of fun. Completing this blanket may lead to bragging rights, a cozy masterpiece, and a suspiciously large pile of leftover yarn.

Follow this journey to create your very own mandala blanket, complete with textures, colors, and beautiful stitches. Use the navigation above to explore each stage.

About Sophie's Universe Crochet Blanket

The Sophie's Universe blanket is a stunning, textured crochet CAL (Crochet-Along) designed by Dedri Uys. Known for its beautiful combination of stitches, intricate patterns, and vibrant colors, this blanket challenges crocheters to expand their skills while creating a cozy masterpiece. From simple motifs to complex layers, each round offers a meditative and rewarding experience, making it a favorite among crochet enthusiasts worldwide.
