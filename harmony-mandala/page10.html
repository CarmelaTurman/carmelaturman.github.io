About the Harmony Circle Mandala

The Harmony Circle Mandala Blanket is a hand-crafted crochet project designed to guide makers through a colorful and meditative journey. Each round introduces unique stitches, textures, and colors, building a vibrant circular design that can expand into a full blanket.

Designed by Carmela Turman, this project is intended for crocheters of all skill levels. The pattern combines traditional crochet techniques with creative colorwork to produce a visually striking and satisfying finished piece.

Carmela Turman is a passionate fiber artist and designer, dedicated to creating projects that inspire relaxation, mindfulness, and creativity. Her work focuses on vibrant color palettes, intricate stitch patterns, and accessible tutorials for makers everywhere.

  • Introduce a variety of crochet stitches in a step-by-step format
  • Provide a meditative and enjoyable crafting experience
  • Create a visually stunning finished blanket suitable for display or gifting
  • Offer a complete set of resources for both beginners and experienced crocheters
