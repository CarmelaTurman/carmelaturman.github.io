Rounds 46–60: Lavender → Aqua → Cream

Rounds 46–50: Lavender
Rounds 51–55: Aqua
Rounds 56–60: Cream

Join Lavender. Ch 4, skip next st, dc in next, ch 2, skip 1; rep around. Join. (≈400 dc)

Ch 1, sc in next dc, ch 3, sc in next dc; rep around. Join. (≈200 ch-3 sps)

Ch 3, 2 dc in next ch-3 sp, ch 1, 3 dc in next sp; rep around. Join. (~600 dc)

Ch 1, sc blo around. Join. (~400 sc)

Ch 3, popcorn in next st, ch 2, skip 1; rep around. Join. (~200 popcorns)

Join Aqua. Ch 2, hdc in each st around. Join. (~400 hdc)

Ch 3, dc in next 2 sts, skip 1, sc, skip 1, dc in next 3; rep around. Join.

Ch 4, skip next 2 sts, sc in next, ch 4; rep around. Join. (~200 ch-4 sps)

Ch 3, 3 dc in each ch-4 sp; rep around. Join. (~600 dc)

Ch 1, sc in each dc blo; rep around. Join. (~600 sc)

Join Cream. Ch 2, hdc in each st around. Join. (~600 hdc)

Ch 3, skip 1, 5 dc in next, skip 1, sc in next; rep around. Join. (~100 petals)

Ch 1, sc between petals, ch 2; rep around. Join. (~100 ch-2 sps)

Ch 2, FPDC around next dc, BPDC around next; rep around. Join. (~400 sts)

Ch 1, sc in next 2 sts, picot (ch 3, sl st in first ch), sc in next 2; rep around. Fasten off, weave ends.
