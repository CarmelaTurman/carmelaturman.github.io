Rounds 31–45: Rose → Teal → Plum

Rounds 31–36: Rose
Rounds 37–42: Teal
Rounds 43–45: Plum

Join Rose. Ch 3, Popcorn in next st, ch 2, skip 1 st; rep around. Join. (≈175 popcorns)

Ch 1, sc in top of each popcorn, sc in next ch-2 sp; rep around. Join. (≈350 sc)

Ch 4 (counts as tr), ch 2, skip 1 st, tr in next; rep around. Join. (≈175 tr)

Ch 3, dc in each ch-2 sp, ch 1; rep around. Join. (≈350 dc)

Ch 1, sc in next dc, ch 4, sc in next dc; rep around. Join. (≈175 ch-4 sps)

Sl st to first ch-4 sp, ch 3, 4 dc in same sp, sc in next ch-4 sp, 5 dc in next ch-4 sp; rep around. (~88 shells)

Join Teal in sc between shells. Ch 1, sc in same, ch 2, sc between next shells; rep around. (~88 sc, 88 ch-2 sps)

Ch 2 (counts as hdc), ch 2, skip next st, hdc in next st; rep around. Join. (≈176 hdc, 176 ch-2 sps)

Ch 3, dc in next st, FPDC around next dc from prev round; rep around. Join. (≈350 dc)

Ch 1, sc in each st around. Join. (~350 sc)

Ch 3, skip 2, 7 dc in next st, skip 2, sc in next; rep around. (~58 large shells)

Ch 1, sc between shells, ch 2; rep around. (≈58 sc, 58 ch-2 sps)

Ch 2, hdc blo in each st around; rep. Join. (~400 hdc blo)

Ch 3, dc in same st, ch 1, dc in same st again, skip 3 sts, (dc, ch1, dc, ch1, dc) in next; rep around. (~133 V-groups)

Ch 3, FPDC around center dc of each V, dc in next st; rep around. (~400 sts)
