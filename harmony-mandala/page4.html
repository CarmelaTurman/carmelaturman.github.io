Rounds 6–10: Harmony Circle Mandala

Follow these instructions to complete Rounds 6–10 of your mandala. Each round builds texture and dimension in your design.

Join Gold in any st. Ch 2 (counts as hdc), ch 1, skip 1, hdc in next st; rep around. Join. (54 total: 27 hdc, 27 ch-1 sps)

Ch 1, sc in same st, ch 2, sc in next hdc; rep around. Join. (54 sc, 54 ch-2 sps)

Ch 3, dc in same st, dc in next 2 sts. 2 dc in next st, dc in next 2 sts; rep around. Join. (72 dc)

Ch 1, Puff st (yo, insert hook, pull up loop) 4 times total, yo, pull through all, ch 1 to close) in next st, skip next st; rep around. Join to top of first puff. (~36 puff sts)

Ch 1, sc between puffs, ch 3; rep around. Join. (36 sc, 36 ch-3 sps)
