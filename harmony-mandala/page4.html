Rounds 6–10: Teal & Aqua

2 dc in each st around; join (~48 dc)

Ch 3, 2 dc in same st, skip 1, 3 dc in next st; rep around (~16 shells)

Popcorn stitch in each shell; join (~16 popcorns)

Ch 4, tr in next; rep around (~48 tr)

Sc in each st around; join (~48 sc)
