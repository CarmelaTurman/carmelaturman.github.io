Rounds 16–30: Aqua → Lavender → Mint

Rounds 16–20: Aqua
Rounds 21–25: Lavender
Rounds 26–30: Mint

Join Aqua in any ch-3 space. Ch 3 (counts as dc), 3dc cluster in same sp, ch 2, 3dc cluster in next ch-3 sp; rep around. Join. (40 clusters)

Ch 1, sc in same st, ch 3, sl st in 1st ch (picot made), sc in next cluster top; rep around. Join. (40 picots)

Ch 2 (counts as hdc), hdc in next 5 sts, 2 hdc in next; rep around. Join. (~280 hdc)

Ch 4 (counts as tr), ch 1, skip 1 st, tr in next; rep around. Join. (~140 tr, 140 ch-1 sps)

Ch 3, dc in same st, ch 2, dc in next st, dc in same st (V), skip 2, (2 dc, ch 2, 2 dc) in next st; rep around. (~94 V sts)

Ch 1, sc in each dc (skip ch spaces). Join. (≈376 sc)

Ch 3, skip next st, dc in next, dc in skipped st; rep around. Join. (≈376 dc)

Ch 1, sc in next st, ch 1, puff st in next st, ch 1; rep around. Join. (~188 puff sts)

Ch 2 (counts as hdc), hdc in each st, 2 hdc every 18 sts; rep around. Join.

Ch 3, skip 2, 5 dc in next, skip 2, sc in next; rep around. Join. (~63 shells)

Join Mint in sc between shells. Ch 1, sc in same, ch 2, sc between next shells; rep around. Join. (~63 sc, 63 ch-2 sps)

Ch 3, V-st (dc, ch 1, dc) in each ch-2 space; rep around. (~126 dc)

Ch 1, sc in V-space, ch 3, sc in next V-space; rep around. Join. (63 sc, 63 ch-3 sps)

Ch 3, dc in back loop only of each st around. Join. (~315 dc)

Ch 2, hdc in next 9 sts, 2 hdc in next; rep around. Join. (~350 hdc)
