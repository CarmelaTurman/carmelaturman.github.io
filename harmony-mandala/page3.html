Rounds 1–5: Harmony Circle Mandala

Follow these instructions to complete the first 5 rounds of your mandala. Each round builds upon the previous one.

Pro tip: If your yarn tries to tie itself in knots, just tell it who's boss… or grab a coffee and breathe. You're officially in Rounds 1–5: Chaos Control Mode!

With Cream, make a magic ring. Ch 3 (counts as dc), 11 dc in ring. Join with sl st to top of ch-3. (12 dc)

Ch 3, dc in same st, 2 dc in each dc around. Join. (24 dc)

Ch 3, ch 1, skip next dc, dc in next dc; rep around. Join. (24 dc, 24 ch-1 sps)

Sl st into next ch sp. Ch 3 (counts as dc), 1 dc in same sp, dc in next dc. 2 dc in next ch sp, dc in next dc; rep around. Join. (36 dc)

Ch 1, sc in back loop only of each dc around. Join. (36 sc)

Warning: Tangled yarn and stubborn stitches may occur. Laugh, swear quietly, then crochet on.
