The Four Seasons

Life moves in rhythm with the turning of the Earth — each season a new chapter in the story of change, color, and renewal.

Spring Awakening

The world yawns awake from its long winter slumber. Petals unfurl like whispered promises, and soft rains kiss the soil awake. Every drop of dew carries hope — fresh, gentle, and alive. The air hums with bees, laughter, and the scent of beginnings.

Summer Radiance

The sun reigns high, spilling gold over fields and oceans alike. Days stretch lazily into twilight, warm and endless. Children chase sunsets, waves dance against the shore, and laughter ripples through the heat. Summer is freedom — bright, bold, and unapologetically alive.

Autumn Reflection

The trees trade green for gold, crimson, and rust — a final burst of brilliance before rest. Leaves swirl like memories in the wind, and the air smells of woodsmoke and endings. Autumn whispers softly that beauty is fleeting, but never forgotten.

Winter’s Embrace

Snow drapes the world in stillness. The air sharpens, and silence deepens, as if time itself pauses to breathe. Yet in the quiet, there is peace — the promise that beneath the frost, life waits, patient and unbroken. Winter reminds us that endings, too, can be beautiful.
